Welcome to TripMa

The traveling industry has been one of the primary income sources in Sri Lanka although with the rapid incensement of digitalization of the world. People tend to use more digital platforms for traveling. The basic drawback of classical planning systems such as organizational trip planning or package-based trip planning is that they are highly dependent on the organizer’s motivations. The classical travel planning methods lack the independent decision-making ability of the user to travel and schedule what they need to occupy. Although the classical planning methodologies provide users expertise and knowledge of the local geography which provides users with an organized touring experience. When we consider the existing trip planning web-related products, many systems used different booking systems and travel information guides but none of them addresses the need for a quality travel experience. When considering the gaps existing in modern trip-related products, the inaccurate mate or tour group assigning can be seen frequently in addition to those there are many systems, which provide travel attraction information but none of them gives an accurate time to visit for a better experience. In addition, there are very few systems have considered the importance of local security-related information. Systems also tend to miss the importance of publishing the accuracy and the user satisfaction rating of the system to enhance the confidence of potential users. Throughout this Research paper, we have implemented a digitalized platform for users to obtain expert knowledge of the local geography and culture with the ability to customize it according to the user’s requirements.
Main components of the system

      Travel buddy suggestion system
      Predicting the best time to visit tourist attractions
      Identification and providing safety issues and relevant precautions to take.
      Traveler Feedback Analysis

Through the initial planning stage, the user will be suggesting a travel companion based on age and transportation medium. In the best visit time analyzer module, we have identified the best time to visit tourist attractions by analyzing real-time crowd data, weather conditions, and the positive ratings of the attraction. The safety analysis module will do Identification of safety ratings for the selected route through a weightage model and predict threatening areas using historical data analysis. In the user feedback analysis, the system analyzes the reviews, identifies the positivity and negativity of the feedback, and displays them in relevant places. Admin can review all the feedback through the dashboard and interact with the people who gave the feedback to improve the system.
